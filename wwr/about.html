Heaven Water Rafting

Our mission is to have a positive impact on people's lives and our natural world by providing outstanding river trips and other outdoor adventure experiences.

History

In the 1950s, Rockefeller used surplus military rafts to offer float trips at his Jackson Lake Lodge in Grand Teton National Park. The first year, fewer than 500 people went on the trips, but demand increased. This attraction was initially considered a failure, but it reflected growing interest in rafting, which led to the continued development of the sport as a commercial attraction.
